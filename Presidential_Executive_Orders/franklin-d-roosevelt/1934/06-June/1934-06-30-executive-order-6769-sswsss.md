## Executive Order 6769

By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by sections 5 and 6 of the act of June 10, 1922 (ch. 212, 42 Stat. 628), as amended by section 3 of the act of March 20, 1933 (ch. 3, 48 Stat. 13), as amended by section 21 of the act of March 28, 1934 (Public, No. 141, 73d Cong.), it is ordered that the following rates be, and they are hereby, made effective for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1935, for the rental and subsistence allowances of officers of the various services entitled thereto:
For one subsistence allowance, 57¢ per day.           For one rental allowance for one room, $19 per month.

FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
The White House,June 30, 1934.
